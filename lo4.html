Career Day

At the Fontys ICT Career Day I saw a board game where they used a very different set up. There is a circle with three different categories. Withing these categories there are three levels. I thought about incorporating this to our design in order to have a more original game. The idea was to have the tree levers be the categories. And within this with each right answer the player gets to move up. At the end of the game this creates a star plot, players can see how well they know each other in each category.
